Freight Truck

Procedural Three.js truck with custom shader materials, dynamic lighting, perspective camera, animated movement, and mouse + keyboard interaction.

Custom Shaders Lighting Textures Animation Interaction
Drive: W/S or Up/Down
Steer: A/D or Left/Right
Brake: Space
Camera: drag with mouse, wheel to zoom, C toggle auto orbit
Light: L toggle rotating inspection light
Horn Smoke: hold H or press the horn button
Reset: R
Auto camera: on | Rotating light: on
